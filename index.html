06 — FAQ

Good to know

Who can apply as a participant?

Young Europeans aged 16–30. No credentials needed — we select for motivation and mix, not CVs.

How much does it cost?

Free, including lunches and materials. Travel is self-funded; selected speakers can apply for a grant.

What language is the forum in?

English. The opening keynote has simultaneous Bulgarian interpretation and is open to everyone in Sofia.

When will I hear back?

Within 10 days. Final participant list by 4 Sep; speakers announced 15 Aug.

Can I attend only one day?

We prefer both — Friday writes the declaration, Saturday delivers it. Sofia locals can request a Day 1 pass.

I want to volunteer or partner

Write to partnerships@betterdemocracy.eu — we especially need Sofia-based volunteers.
